## Research Overview

My research focuses on the **mechanical behavior and long-term performance of FRP–concrete hybrid structures**, integrating:

- Large-scale structural experiments
- Finite element modeling (Abaqus + user subroutines)
- Analytical derivations
- Design-oriented structural methods

The goal is to develop **durable and efficient composite structural systems for bridge engineering applications**.
